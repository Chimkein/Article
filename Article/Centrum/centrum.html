CTU Facility Centrum

A Hub of Learning and Community

The Cebu Technological University Facility Centrum, located within the Danao Campus, stands as a cornerstone of academic and community life in Danao City. This multi-functional facility serves as a hub for various activities, from academic pursuits to social events.

Community Hub

The Facility Centrum is more than just an academic building; it is also a vibrant community hub. The facility often hosts various events, including cultural performances, sports competitions, and social gatherings. These events foster a sense of belonging and camaraderie among the CTU Danao community.
