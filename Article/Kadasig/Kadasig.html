CTU Danao Kadasig Gym: A Haven for Fitness and Wellness

The Cebu Technological University Danao Campus has recently enriched its facilities with the addition of the Kadasig Gym. This state-of-the-art gym is a testament to the university's commitment to providing a holistic education that includes physical and mental well-being.

A Community Hub for Fitness

Beyond individual workouts, the Kadasig Gym serves as a hub for the CTU Danao community. It's a place where students can connect, engage in group fitness activities, and participate in sports competitions. The gym's open and inviting atmosphere fosters a sense of camaraderie and support among its members.
