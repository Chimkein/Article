The CTU Sports Complex: A Hub for Athletic Excellence and Community Engagement

The Cebu Technological University (CTU) Sports Complex stands as a beacon of athletic excellence and community engagement in the heart of Danao City. This state-of-the-art facility not only serves the students of CTU but also welcomes local residents to participate in a variety of sports and recreational activities.

Facilities and Amenities

The CTU Sports Complex is designed to cater to a wide range of sports, providing top-notch facilities for basketball, volleyball, badminton, and track and field, among others. Its spacious gymnasium is equipped with modern equipment and offers ample space for training and competitions. The track and field area, with its well-maintained running tracks, provides athletes with an ideal environment to train and improve their performance.
